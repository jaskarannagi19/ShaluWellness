Yoga

Get ready to embrace a forever evolving practice that activates physical strength, flexibility and endurance for the mind and body. This practice will relieve physical, mental and emotional tensions, allowing you to experience a sense of calm and tranquility that you can take with you off your Yoga mat. This practice is a pathway to tap into a deeper part of who we really are, it ignites our purpose and passions leading us to live a more fulfilling high quality life.

Reiki

Reiki is a Japanese alternative energy medicine technique that promotes the body's relaxation and natural healing ability. Reiki dissolves emotional illusions and empowers you to reveal your authentic self. This gentle treatment is offered in single or multiple sessions and is suitable for anyone open to a transformational experience.

Law of Attraction Life Coaching

Well Being is more than being free from illness: it is a process of transformation and growth towards a mentally and physically healthy lifestyle. To achieve optimal well being, one must apply it towards every possible endeavor. You can apply a wellness approach towards your environment, community, career, belief systems, physical activities, self care, healthy eating, self esteem, and creative activities. Applying wellness in your everyday life will allow you to achieve your full potential and live with freedom, happiness and purpose.

Meditation and Yoga for Young Ones

While we are all young at heart, developing and nurturing our minds and bodies when we are physically young can last a lifetime. Teaching children that they have control over their emotions and reactions and how to take meditation or stretch breaks can be the relief they need to be fully present to the learning and fulfilling life that is available to them. Stop, Focus and Breath can be one of the greatest gifts you give a child.
